Justice Made Simple

Track your case status in real-time and file complaints anonymously. We're making the Indian judicial system more accessible, transparent, and citizen-friendly.

What Is This System?

Think of it as your personal assistant for navigating the legal system. Instead of running from pillar to post or waiting in long queues, you can now track your court case from your phone or computer. And if you've witnessed something wrong but fear speaking up? We've got you covered with completely anonymous filing.

📋

Automated Case Tracking

No more confusion about hearing dates or case status. Just enter your case number and get instant updates — next hearing date, case progress, judge details, and more. It's like tracking your food delivery, but for justice!

🔒

Anonymous Case Filing

Worried about retaliation? We understand. File complaints about corruption, misconduct, or illegal activities without revealing your identity. Your voice matters, and we protect it.

About This Project

This project was developed as part of a DevOps course demonstration, showcasing modern web development practices including version control with Git, clean code structure, and responsive design principles.

The concept addresses a real problem faced by millions of Indians — the complexity of tracking legal cases and the fear of filing complaints. While this is a demonstration project, it represents the potential for technology to make justice more accessible to everyone.
