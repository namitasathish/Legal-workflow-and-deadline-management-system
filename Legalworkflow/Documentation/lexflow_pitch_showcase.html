The problem

Indian lawyers are running
complex cases on chaos

Most solo practitioners and small firms in India manage casework through a patchwork of tools that were never designed for legal work.

📋
No single source of truth
Case notes in notebooks, deadlines in phone calendars, client info in contacts. Nothing speaks to anything else.
⏰
Missed hearing deadlines
Urgent hearings buried in a flat list with no visual urgency. "Mehta v. State is tomorrow" should never be a surprise.
💸
Fee chaos & unpaid bills
Fee requests sent on WhatsApp, payment status tracked in memory. Outstanding amounts have no visibility.
📱
Client left in the dark
Clients call repeatedly asking "what's happening?" because they have no way to independently track their own case.
🌐
Cloud-dependent apps fail
Court complexes in India often have poor connectivity. Cloud-first apps become useless exactly when you need them most.
🔒
No confidentiality control
Sensitive client data sitting in generic cloud tools that were never designed for attorney-client privilege.

Under the hood

Built to survive courtrooms,
not just conference rooms

Every architectural decision was made with the realities of Indian legal practice in mind — poor connectivity, sensitive data, and zero tolerance for downtime.

📱
React Native + Expo
Cross-platform iOS & Android from a single codebase. Expo handles camera, file system, and font loading natively.
🗄️
Local SQLite database
Fully offline-capable relational database. Cases, tasks, documents, messages, payments — all work without a network.
🔐
Salted password hashing
Multi-round salted hashing via crypto.js. Passwords are never stored as plaintext. Legacy migration handled gracefully.
🔄
useFocusEffect refresh
Every screen re-queries the database on focus. Badges, counts, and chat logs are always perfectly in sync — no stale state.
📅
Native date/time pickers
@react-native-community/datetimepicker replaces all text date inputs. No invalid dates, better UX, platform-native feel.
⚡
Deadline engine
Mathematical scan of all scheduled dates on every dashboard load. Surfaces "Urgent" (3 days) and "This Week" (7 days) automatically.
🛣️
Dual-stack navigation
Lawyer and client screen stacks are physically separated in App.js. Role determines which navigator mounts — no shared routes.
💬
5-second chat polling
Near-real-time messaging without a WebSocket server. SQLite polling every 5 seconds — works entirely offline and on local networks.
📲
Haptics + animations
expo-haptics on every action, slide_from_right navigation transitions, pull-to-refresh on all lists, and animated stat counters.
